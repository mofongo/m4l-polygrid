polygrid

An 8-track polymeter step sequencer for Ableton Live.

Every row is its own loop — draw a block anywhere in the bar, give it 3, 5, or 8 steps, and it cycles there forever.

Eight loops against one bar

polygrid locks a one-bar master window to Live's transport, then lets every track ignore it in its own way: each row has an independent step count, loop length, and phase offset — a sequencing model inspired by Sonogrid, the iOS polymeter sequencer by Rosano, sadly no longer on the App Store due to Apple's store policies.

Track

Note

Each row plays one pitch. Nudge it by semitones or octaves — melodies emerge from the stack, not the row.

Track

Steps

1 to 32 steps per row. Click pills to toggle, Push/Pop to grow or shrink, Dbl/Half to re-subdivide the same loop.

Timing

Length + Offset

Drag the block to shift its phase, grab an edge to resize. Everything snaps to 1/16 of the bar.

Mix

Mute · Solo

Per-track mute and solo, so you can peel the stack apart and rebuild it live.

Presets Gallery

Hit Save and the whole grid — all eight tracks, notes, lengths, offsets, mutes — becomes a thumbnail in the gallery. Every preset renders as a miniature of its pattern, so you browse by shape. Click a thumbnail and it loads instantly; the ✕ in its corner deletes it. Presets and the working grid both save with your Live set.
